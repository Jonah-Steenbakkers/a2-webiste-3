Facts on Larry Page


Both of Larry Pages Parents were very involved with computers.

Gloria Page, Larry's mother had a degree in mathematics and with the help of her husband, Carl, she had become a master when it comes to computer science.
His father, Carl Page was a professer at the University of Michigan where he taught computer science.
It is no suprise why Larry was so succesful in the computer science industry when you consider his parents.


Larry Page has made a lot of money over the course of his carrer in the computer science industry, and he is not alone. Page is ranked among the richest in the world with other extremely succesful and rich computer scientists/company founders like Bill Gates (Microsoft) and Jeff Bezos (Amazon).

Forbes had him ranked as the 24th richest person in the world back in 2010. 10 years later, in 2020, he is worth about 56 billion USD.
He is also currently ranked as the 6th richest person in the USA.


When Larry Page and Sergey Brin were in university developing their search engine, they rented out a garage to work in.
